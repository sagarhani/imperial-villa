WEDDING PARTY

RECEPTIONS From the linens, menu and seating chart, down to the flowers and other personal touches, DoubleTree is here to help you coordinate the details of your wedding reception. Enjoy the Party Whether you’re thinking small poolside cocktail party or formal candlelit dinner, DoubleTree by Hilton™ can provide the ideal setting for the wedding reception you’ve been imagining. Our modern yet warm design creates a perfect backdrop for friends and family to have an unforgettable time. Find a location for your wedding reception today. Eat Well Before you hit the dance floor, please raise your glass and enjoy a delicious reception dinner with your friends and loved ones. DoubleTree catering teams will work with you to create a customized menu to complement your unique style. Sleep Well A warm chocolate chip cookie upon arrival is just the beginning of the treats we have in store. We invite you to settle in to one of our stylish, comfortable guest rooms or Honeymoon Suites. Our Sweet Dreams® by DoubleTree Sleep Experience features high-thread count linens, cozy down bedding, elegant bedcovers and a custom plush-top mattress for total relaxation. You’ll also find in-room HD flat screen televisions and CITRON by Crabtree & Evelyn toiletries in many of our 250 locations. We hope you’ll find that each room is your private retreat. Wedding Reception Tips A little variety on the reception menu goes a long way. Consider adding an innovative vegetarian or vegan option for guests with special dietary needs.
